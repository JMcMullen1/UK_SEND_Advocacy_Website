About UK SEND Advocacy — Expert SEN Advocates

Combining professional SEND legal expertise with personal understanding to fight for every child's right to education and the SEN support they deserve.

Our Story

UK SEND Advocacy was founded with a clear mission: to make quality education advocacy affordable, accessible, and authoritative for every family of a child with special educational needs and disabilities in England.

We know first-hand how overwhelming the SEND system can be. The legal complexity of the Children and Families Act 2014, the emotional strain of fighting for your child's educational rights, and the feeling of being up against a local authority that should be supporting you — we've seen it all, and we're here to change that experience for families.

Our team of experienced SEN advocates combines professional SEND legal knowledge with personal experience of navigating the SEND system as parents and carers ourselves. We understand SEND law, the SEND Code of Practice, and the Equality Act 2010, but we also understand what it feels like to fight for a child with autism, ADHD, dyslexia, or other SEN who isn't getting the support they need. That combination drives everything we do.

Our Mission

To empower families to navigate the SEND system and secure the right education, SEN support, and provision for their children — through expert education advocacy that is affordable, compassionate, and effective.

Accessible to All

Every family deserves access to quality education advocacy. We keep our prices fair and transparent so that expert SEND support isn't reserved only for those who can afford SEN solicitor fees.

Children First

Every decision we make, every document we draft, and every argument we present is driven by one goal: securing the best possible outcome for your child.

Empowering Families

We don't just fight battles for families — we equip parents and carers with the knowledge and confidence to self-advocate for their child's educational rights, through our education advocacy services and our SEND Advocacy Academy.

Our Values

Integrity

We are always honest with families about the strength of their case, the likely outcomes, and what we can realistically achieve. We never oversell or make false promises.

Compassion

We understand the emotional weight of fighting for your child's education. Our advocates provide not just professional expertise, but genuine empathy and emotional support.

Excellence

We hold ourselves to the highest professional standards. Every document, every piece of advice, and every representation is meticulously prepared and delivered.

Fairness

Quality advocacy should not be a privilege. We are committed to transparent, fair pricing and to finding ways to support families regardless of their financial circumstances.

Our Expertise

Our SEN advocates bring deep expertise across the full spectrum of SEND law, education advocacy, and children's educational rights:

  • IPSEA trained with ongoing professional development
  • Extensive knowledge of the Children and Families Act 2014, SEND Code of Practice, and Equality Act 2010
  • Experience with all types of SEND Tribunal appeals
  • Knowledge of disability discrimination claims and reasonable adjustments in education settings
  • Understanding of local authority processes across all English councils
  • Personal experience of the SEND system as parents and carers
