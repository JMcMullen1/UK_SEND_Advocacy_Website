About UK SEND Advocacy

Combining professional legal expertise with personal understanding to fight for every child's right to education.

Our Story

UK SEND Advocacy was founded with a clear mission: to make quality SEND advocacy affordable, accessible, and authoritative for every family in England.

We know first-hand how overwhelming the SEND system can be. The legal complexity, the emotional strain, and the feeling of being up against a system that should be supporting you — we've seen it all, and we're here to change that experience for families.

Our team combines professional legal knowledge with personal experience of the SEND system. We understand the law, but we also understand what it feels like to fight for a child who isn't getting the support they need. That combination drives everything we do.

Our Mission

To empower families to navigate the SEND system and secure the right education and support for their children — through expert advocacy that is affordable, compassionate, and effective.

Accessible to All

Every family deserves access to quality advocacy. We keep our prices fair and transparent so that expert support isn't reserved only for those who can afford solicitor fees.

Children First

Every decision we make, every document we draft, and every argument we present is driven by one goal: securing the best possible outcome for your child.

Empowering Families

We don't just fight battles for families — we equip them with the knowledge and confidence to advocate for themselves, through our services and our SEND Academy.

Our Values

Integrity

We are always honest with families about the strength of their case, the likely outcomes, and what we can realistically achieve. We never oversell or make false promises.

Compassion

We understand the emotional weight of fighting for your child's education. Our advocates provide not just professional expertise, but genuine empathy and emotional support.

Excellence

We hold ourselves to the highest professional standards. Every document, every piece of advice, and every representation is meticulously prepared and delivered.

Fairness

Quality advocacy should not be a privilege. We are committed to transparent, fair pricing and to finding ways to support families regardless of their financial circumstances.

Our Expertise

Our advocates bring deep expertise across the full spectrum of SEND law and education rights:

  • IPSEA trained with ongoing professional development
  • Extensive knowledge of the Children and Families Act 2014 and SEND Code of Practice
  • Experience with all types of SEND Tribunal appeals
  • Knowledge of disability discrimination in education settings
  • Understanding of local authority processes across all English councils
  • Personal experience of the SEND system as parents and carers
