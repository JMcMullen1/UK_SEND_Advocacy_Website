Our SEND Advocacy Services

Independent, expert advice and advocacy across all aspects of SEND law for children and young people aged 0 to 25 in England.

Comprehensive Support at Every Stage

Whether you're requesting an initial assessment or preparing for a tribunal hearing, we provide expert advocacy tailored to your family's needs.

EHCNA Requests

An Education, Health and Care Needs Assessment (EHCNA) is the essential first step in obtaining an EHCP for your child. The strength of the initial request can determine whether your local authority agrees to assess.

We help you draft a compelling and comprehensive request that clearly demonstrates your child's needs, referencing the relevant legal tests and supporting evidence to maximise the chances of success.

  • Review of existing reports and evidence
  • Drafting a legally robust EHCNA request letter
  • Guidance on gathering supporting evidence
  • Follow-up support and next steps advice

Refusal to Assess Appeals

If your local authority refuses to carry out an EHC needs assessment, you have the right to appeal to the First-tier Tribunal (Special Educational Needs and Disability). We can help you exercise that right effectively.

Our advocates prepare all necessary documents to submit the appeal, including reviewing and advising on law, evidence, and tactics. We provide continued advice and support throughout the appeal process.

  • Full preparation of appeal documentation
  • Legal advice on merits and strategy
  • Liaison with Tribunal, school, and local authority
  • Ongoing support until resolution
Learn About Appeals

EHCP Content Appeals

If your child has an EHCP but it doesn't accurately describe their needs or the provision required, you can appeal the content of the plan. Getting the detail right is crucial — it determines the support your child receives every day.

We prepare all documents required to submit the appeal, including drafting the Grounds of Appeal and advising on the Working Document, with continued support up to the date the Final Hearing Bundle is due.

  • Detailed analysis of EHCP against legal requirements
  • Drafting comprehensive Grounds of Appeal
  • Working Document amendments and advice
  • Support through to Final Hearing Bundle

Tribunal Representation

When your case reaches the SEND Tribunal, having an experienced advocate by your side can make all the difference. We provide expert attendance and representation at tribunal hearings across England.

Our advocates present your case with authority, cross-examine witnesses effectively, and ensure your child's voice is heard where it matters most. We handle all preparation in the lead-up to the hearing date.

  • Full hearing preparation and case presentation
  • Expert cross-examination of LA witnesses
  • In-person attendance at hearings nationwide
  • Post-hearing follow-up and compliance
