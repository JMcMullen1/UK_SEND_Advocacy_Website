Frequently Asked Questions About SEND Advocacy

An Education, Health and Care Plan (EHCP) is a legal document for children and young people aged 0 to 25 who need more support than is available through standard SEN provision in schools. It sets out your child's special educational needs, the provision required to meet those needs, and the outcomes to be achieved. Crucially, an EHCP provides legally enforceable rights to the specific support your child needs — meaning the local authority must ensure that provision is delivered.
